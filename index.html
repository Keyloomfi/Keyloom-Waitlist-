The Keyloom Please Don't Do This List 🚨

Before you close, we're begging you:

  • 🚫Do not quit your job. Not even if your boss is insufferable.
  • 🚫Do not buy a boat. Or a car. Or a jet ski. Or anything with a motor.
  • 🚫Do not open a new credit card no matter how good the rewards sound.
  • 🚫Do not move your money around like you're hiding it from someone.
  • 🚫Do not make any large deposits without a paper trail. Yes, even from your mom.
  • 🚫Do not co-sign anyone else's loan. Not your best friend. Not your cousin. Nobody.
  • 🏠And please — buy a home with enough left over to at least get a couch. We'll make sure of it.

And the one nobody tells you until it's too late:

  • ⚠️Do not fall in love with a payment without knowing your full payment. Property taxes and homeowners insurance can turn your $2,500 mortgage into a $5,000 reality check real fast. We show you the full number upfront — because finding out after closing is how people end up selling a house they just bought.
